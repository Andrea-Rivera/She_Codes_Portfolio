Biography

I am Andrea Rivera. I studied Electronic Engineering in Colombia.

I graduated and then started a career in the telecommunications industry, providing technical support for an internet-provider company. I provided technical assistance to customers.

I applied for the skill migration program in Australia as a technician in electronics, ultimately obtaining a resident visa. In 2012, I came to Australia. I embraced the opportunity to work as an electronic assembler, where I follow diagrams and solder components and tested intricate circuits.

As time passed, a desire for new challenges and personal growth ignited within me. In 2017, I made the decision to transition from hardware to software and I enrolled in a computer science course .

Shortly after I graduated in 2019, I received the most delightful surprise, I discovered that we were expecting a little bundle of joy. However, as I joyously anticipated her new role as a mother, an unexpected visitor named Covid arrived uninvited, turning my world upside down.

As the world slowly recovers and my baby blossoms into a curious and independent toddler, I have decided to rekindle my career as a web developer. I have embarked on a course with Code Like a Girl, focusing on HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React.

I am currently undertaking a course with She Codes, where I aim to expand my expertise in front-end technologies and use Python for back-end development. With each passing day, I am growing into a more proficient and versatile web developer, ready to make my mark in this dynamic industry.

As I embrace this new chapter in my professional journey, I am fueled by resilience, dedication, and an unwavering commitment to excellence. The challenges I have faced along the way have only strengthened my resolve. With a steadfast determination and a enthusiasm to learm more. I am ready to shape the digital landscape, leaving an indelible mark as a skilled and innovative web developer.
