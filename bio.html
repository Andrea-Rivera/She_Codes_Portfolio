Biography

Allow me to introduce myself. I am Andrea Rivera, I am from Colombia. From an early age, I found myself drawn to the captivating realm of electronics. I dedicated my time and energy to studying electronics in my homeland. I enjoyed learning about the intricacies of circuits, transistors, and resistors.

After years of dedication and hard work, I graduated and then I started a career in the telecommunications industry, providing technical support for an internet-provider company. I provided technical assistance to customers. However, a longing for new experiences and personal growth led me to make a life-changing decision.

I set my sights on Australia, a land known for its vibrant culture, adorable kangaroos, and breathtaking landscapes. Eager to explore this captivating country and enhance my English proficiency, I embarked on a journey to study the language. Upon my return to Colombia, my heart remained captivated by Australia's allure, prompting me to take a leap of faith.

I applied for the skill migration program as a technician in electronics, ultimately obtaining a resident visa. In 2012, I returned Australia, and commenced a new chapter in my life. I embraced the opportunity to work as an electronic assembler, where I follow diagrams and solder components and tested intricate circuits.

As time passed, a desire for new challenges and personal growth ignited within me. In 2017, I made the decision to transition from hardware to software, venturing into a remarkable journey in the field of computer science. With enthusiasm, I enrolled in a course, eager to unlock the secrets of coding, and the boundless possibilities of software development.

As fate would have it, this newfound passion coincided with my desire to start a family with my loving husband. Shortly after I graduated in 2019, I received the most delightful surprise, I discovered that we were expecting a little bundle of joy. However, as I joyously anticipated her new role as a mother, an unexpected visitor named Covid arrived uninvited, turning my world upside down.

The advent of the Covid-19 pandemic brought about unprecedented challenges and uncertainties. With little knowledge of the virus and its potential impact on my baby, I made the selfless decision to prioritize the well-being of my family and put my career on hold. I dedicated my time and energy to creating a safe and nurturing environment during these trying times.

As the world slowly recovers and my baby blossoms into a curious and independent toddler, I have decided to rekindle my career as a web developer. I have embarked on a course with Code Like a Girl, focusing on HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React‚Äîessential tools in the world of web development.

I am currently undertaking a course with She Codes, where I aim to expand my expertise in front-end technologies and use Python for back-end development. With each passing day, I am growing into a more proficient and versatile web developer, ready to make my mark in this dynamic industry.

As I embrace this new chapter in my professional journey, I am fueled by resilience, dedication, and an unwavering commitment to excellence. The challenges I have faced along the way have only strengthened my resolve. With a steadfast determination and a enthusiasm to learm more. I am ready to shape the digital landscape, leaving an indelible mark as a skilled and innovative web developer.
